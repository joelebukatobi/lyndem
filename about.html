About Us

Lyndem is an interactive educational centre where children are encouraged to discover their intellectual abilities through the art of gameplay. We expose them to educational games of different sorts which challenges their innate desire to learn and stir up their interest in a variety of topics.

Mission

The main purpose of edutainment is to promote learning through having fun. Our main goal is to infuse fun into the learning process making it an enjoyable experience for the kids so they become eager learners

Lyndem Edutainment is an educational service provider that engenders learning through the art of interactive play using educational props and activities
